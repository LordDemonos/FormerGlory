## Raiding Level Requirements

- **Old World Planes**
	- Level 46+
- **Kunark Targets**
	- Level 50+
- **Veeshan's Peak**
	- Level 56 (Nov '24)
    - Level 57 (Dec '24)
    - Level 58 (Jan '25)
    - Level 59 (Feb '25)
    - Level 60 (Mar '25)

## Raiding Gear Requirements

### Veeshan's Peak Raids
- **Resist Gear:**
  - Magic Resist
  - Fire Resist
  - Disease Resist
- **Consumables:**
  - Resist Potions (available from Shaman)
  - Pumice Stone (available from vendors)

### Kunark Dragon Raids
- **Resist Gear:**
  - Magic Resist
  - Fire Resist
  - Cold Resist
  - Poison Resist
- **Consumables:**
  - At least 8-10 Bark Potions (available from vendors)

### Plane of Fear
- **Resist Gear:**
  - Disease Resist
  - Magic Resist

### Plane of Sky
- **Resist Gear:**
  - Disease Resist
