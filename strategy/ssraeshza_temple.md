Ssraeshza Temple can be confusing to navigate for those unfamiliar with the zone. There are multiple floors that are connected to each other via tubes. Below you can see how to get to each boss.

Main Floor

![ssra-main-floor](/assets/img/ssra-main-floor.jpg)

**Bosses on Main Floor:**

- **[Rhag'Zhezum (Rhag 1)](/strategy/rhag`zhezum)** - Located in the bottom left area of the main floor.
<br/>

	- **Hits:** 400+
	- **Immune to Slow**
	- **Flurries**
	- **Undead**
	- **Casts:** *Rhag’Zhezum’s Deathly Embrace* (Single Target, 0.5 second stun, -100 endurance/tick, -300 Cold Resist)
	- **Low HP**

Second Floor

You'll notice there are two locked doors marked Zazush Key and Zherozsh Key. These keys are uncommon drops off Commander Zazush and Commander Zherozh, respectively. These doors cannot be Pick Locked otherwise.

![ssra-floor-2](/assets/img/ssra-floor-2.jpg)

**Bosses on Second Floor:**

- **[Rhag'Mozdezh (Rhag 2)](/strategy/rhag`mozdezh)** - Located in the bottom right area, accessible via the second floor.

	- **Hits:** 800+
	- **Cannot be Slowed**
	- **Rampages**
	- **Undead**
	- **Casts:** *Breath of Rhag'Mozdezh* (PBAE, -275 HP/Tick DoT and -100 Endurance Drain, -150 Disease Based, 9 counter Disease Cure)

Third Floor

![ssra-floor-3](/assets/img/ssra-floor-3.jpg)

**Bosses on Third Floor:**

- **[Arch Lich Rhag'Zadune (Rhag 3)](/strategy/arch_lich_rhag`zadune)** - Final boss of the spawn cycle, requires Zazuzh Key to access.

	- **Hits:** 900+
	- **Immune to Slow**
	- **Rampages**
	- **Undead**
	- **Casts:** *Annual Self* (Self Dispel)
	- **Casts:** *Deathly Chants* (PBAE, Lowers Mana Pool by 1500, -120 HP/Tick DoT, Unresistable, 9 Curse Counter)
	- **Casts:** *Death Shackles* (Single Target, -300 DD / 8 second stun, pushback, -50 endurance, Unresistable)
	- **High HP (Estimate 1 million)**

- **[Xerkizh The Creator](/strategy/xerkizh_the_creator)** - Located on the third floor side that requires the Zherozsh Key.

	- **Hits:** 900+
	- **Slowable**
	- **Rampages**
	- **Perma-rooted**
	- **Casts:** *Weakening Spray* (PBAE, -300 Attack/AC + Small DoT, -200 Poison Based)
	- **Casts:** *Balance of Zebuxoruk* (PBAE, -75% Healing, 9 curse counters, Unresistable)
	- **High HP:** (Estimated 700k–900k; not confirmed)
	- **Casts Various Minor DD/DoT/Malo Spells**

- **[Vyzh`dra the Cursed](/strategy/vyzhdra_the_cursed)** - Located on the third floor side that requires the Zherozsh Key.

  - **Hits:** 800+
  - **Estimated HP:** 1.2 million
  - **Slowable**
  - **Flurries**
  - **Casts:** *Mass Insanity* (PBAE, Charm, Disease Based)
  - **Casts:** *Caustic Mist* (PBAE, 500 HP/Tick DoT, -300 Poison Based)

- **[High Priest of Ssraeshza](/strategy/high_priest_of_ssraeshza)** - Located on the third floor side that requires the Zherozsh Key.

	- **Hits:** 1,000+
	- **Immune to Slow**
	- **Perma-rooted**
	- **Rampages**
	- **Casts:** *Ssraeshza’s Command* (Targeted AE, 9 second stun + 100 DD, -200 Magic Based)
	- **Casts:** *Sermon of Ssraeshza* (Single Target, 300 DD / 0.5 Sec Stun / -375 AC, -150 Magic Based)
	- **Casts:** *Reckoning* (Single Target, 600 DD, Magic Based)
	- **Fairly High Hit Points:** (Estimated 700k–900k; not confirmed)

Basement

![ssra-basement-map](/assets/img/ssra-basement-map.jpg)

**Events in Basement:**

- Cursed Event - Located in the upper left area of the basement.

Emperor Floor

![ssra-emp](/assets/img/ssra-emp.jpg)

**Bosses on Emperor Floor:**

- **[Emperor Ssraeshza](/strategy/emperor_ssraeshza)** - Final boss of Ssraeshza Temple, accessible via Pick Lock from the main floor.
