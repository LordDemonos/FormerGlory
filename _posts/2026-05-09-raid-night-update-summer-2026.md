## Raid Night Update

I realized this morning I just put out a recruitment post advertising Saturday raids. We've been having attendance issues for a few weeks now and Saturdays suffer the most. Instead of constantly forcing people to switch to alts to make these raids happen, I'd rather give people a night back to go out and enjoy themselves, hopefully preventing any additional burnout. I do not make this decision lightly. My attendance should show I'm a raider at heart and would prefer a 4 day schedule. 

**Starting the week of May 11th we will switch to a 3 day raiding schedule. Lord Inquisitor Seru will move to Friday after Aten Ha Ra. If we have time, we will assess additional targets. Saturday May 9th will be the last Saturday raid until further notice. DKP earned each raid tick will remain at 25 per hour. This means you will have less opportunity to earn DKP each week.**

## When do Saturdays return?

I think its supply and demand. When demand increases, we'll supply with more DKP and raiding. We're averaging about 45 on weeknights and Saturdays have dropped below 30. Sub 30 is clearly the danger zone for successful raiding unless we take out off night type targets. So in order to have confidence in adding Saturday back on the schedule, I'd like to see weeknights exceed 60 again. When that happens, feel free to vocalize your interest in Saturday raiding. Leadership will then re-assess the situation and see if Saturdays is right for us once again. I don't have any immediate plans of Planes of Power, but it may be wise to continue a lighter schedule until elemental planes are out and the majority of our crew are Lv65 with some PoP AAs. Until then, we'll keep recruiting and try to bring up new members. I'm hoping Planes of Power sees some old faces return for the grind and new content. Luclin was very much released poorly and was always doomed to be a tourist/raid logging expansion. 

Thank you all for your cooperation in this. My DMs are always open if you wish to discuss this further, or if you have any ideas on how we can improve. Xanax loves and appreciates you all!
